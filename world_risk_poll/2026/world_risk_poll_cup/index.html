The World Risk Poll Cup!

A playful tournament built from real World Risk Poll data.
Here's the game: pick a survey question, then pick the answer you reckon your country leads the world on. All 48 World Cup nations* then battle it out, group stage, knockouts, the final, to see who really tops it. A country's score is simply the share of its people who gave that answer, and the highest share wins each match. Can you find the answer that carries your team to the trophy?

* The draw fields 48 teams to fill a standard group stage. Of these, 43 have public World Risk Poll data; the other five (Qatar, Iran, Haiti, Curaçao and Cape Verde) aren't in this dataset, so they appear as placeholders and forfeit every match (counted as an automatic loss). The poll itself covers around 150,000 people across 140+ countries; this game uses only the 43 nations with comparable public results across the questions shown.
